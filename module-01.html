Overview

What You'll Accomplish

Before you write a single word of your book, you need to know what kind of writer you are. This free assessment uses a genre personality quiz to match you to the book type that best fits your natural voice, interests, and strengths.

Understanding your genre is the foundation of everything that follows — your concept, your structure, your readers, your cover, your marketing. Start here.

Module 1 Deliverable

Your genre personality result, a clear understanding of your ideal book type, and the confidence to move into Module 2 with a defined direction.

What You'll Learn

How This Works

Take the Genre Quiz Below

Answer honestly. There are no right or wrong answers — only your answers. Your result will point you toward the genre that matches your natural instincts as a writer and the type of book you are most likely to finish and publish successfully.

The quiz takes approximately 5–8 minutes. When you finish, read your result carefully. Your genre profile includes recommended structure models, comp title suggestions, and the core conceptual challenge your genre presents to first-time authors.
